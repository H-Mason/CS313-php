North American male deer (also known as a buck) usually weigh 68 to 136 kg (150 to 300 lb),[9] but mature bucks over 180 kg (400 lb) have been recorded.The female (doe) in North America usually weighs from 40 to 90 kg (88 to 198 lb).Length ranges from 95 to 220 cm (37 to 87 in), including a tail of 10 to 37 cm (3.9 to 14.6 in), and the shoulder height is 53 to 120 cm (21 to 47 in).
